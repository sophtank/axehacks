Welcome to MeloMetrics.

This is a project completed for the 2024 GirlsWhoCode AxeHacks event. Our project was data visualization, and we chose to visualize Spotify data of music popularity from the 2000s to now.

Plans

Originally, we wanted to look at datasets and compare Apple Music usage with Spotify usage. However, we were not able to access Apple Music data and had to change plans.

We split into two groups -- three people would create Tableau visualizations of data that we sourced through Kaggle, and two people would work on the website.

Our goals by the end of the hackathon were to:

  1. Compare and contrast music trends from the early 2000s to modern day.
  2. Create a a line graph representing growth of Spotify users over timeline of data gathered.
  3. Create a website to show our visualizations.
  4. Record a short video showcasing our final product.
  5. Reflect on cultural changes since 2000.
